How To Use?

1. Add an Item

Add an item or ingredient to the list you would like the scan to detect from the picture you take.

2. Scan!

Hit the 'Scan' button after you add the items/ingredients to the list. Take a picture of the ingredients list from the product's label/package, and the scan result will tell you whether or not the items/ingredients you added to the list are present in the product, according to the product's ingredients.

Important

Make sure to take the clearest picture as possible. Any blurry or 'shaky' photos/images might not be read properly by the app.
